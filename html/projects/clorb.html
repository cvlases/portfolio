// understand

Shared-household chore tools often assume the issue is remembering tasks. Clorb starts from a different insight: the hard part is emotional friction, accountability, and the weird interpersonal weight of asking people to do their part.

// define

The product reframes chore management as a social system. Instead of a sterile to-do list, Clorb creates an environment where household work feels playful enough to keep using.

// develop

Technically, Clorb used a four-table Supabase schema, anonymous auth, and real-time presence, with a React/Vite frontend and Vercel deployment. It reads well because it is both a design story and an actual shipped build.
